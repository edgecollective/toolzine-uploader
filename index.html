FarmHack ToolZine Uploader

If you've already made a paper-based 'tool zine' using 8.5x11'' paper (using the format shown below), you can upload a photo of your zine to this site, and process it into an web-based tutorial!

       

1. Upload

Upload a photo of your zine (landscape-oriented).


2. Crop

Crop the uploaded photo to the original paper size.



3. Add notes

Annotate your zine!



4. Save & Share

Click 'save' to save your notes and generate an online version of your zine!
